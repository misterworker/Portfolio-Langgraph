Overview - Workout Tracker

An Android mobile application developed for fitness lovers - Designed for an intuitive and satisfactory way of recording 
your workouts and analysing your progress metrics. Developed Solo.
Features: Theme Switching, Authentication with Firebase, CRUD functions for History, Workouts and Exercises, Premium features, 
and Data Visualisations

Technologies used: Android Studio, Kotlin, Firebase, Google Cloud Run, Google Play Developer APIs
